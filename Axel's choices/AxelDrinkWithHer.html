Drink with her

Axel nods, grabbing them both a glass. He pours her drink first, then grabs his. He takes a sip, then another customer calls him over and he goes to handle it. When he comes back he smiles at the woman, taking another drink. He can immediatly tell something's wrong. His head feels heavy, his vision blurring. His vison goes black, the woman's face the last thing he sees.

Axel blinks awake a bit later, his head aching. He can see the woman from the inn walking around, an older man bent over a bench. They notice he's awake, walking over to him.
