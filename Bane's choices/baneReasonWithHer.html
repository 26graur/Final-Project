Try to reason with her

Bane dodges Pax's attacks, trying to reason with her. She doesn't stop attacking until he mentions her parents, asking if she wants to die in here like they did. She stops attacking, looking up at him. She asks what he means and he explains that he overheard Teric talking about how he'd made sure Pax's parents died in the arena. Pax turns and runs out of the arena.

Later Bane meets Axel outside later, all the gladiators now free. Pax comes out a bit later, covered in blood. Her voice is cold as she says Teric is dead.
