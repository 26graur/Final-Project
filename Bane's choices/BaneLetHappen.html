Let it happen

The wagon travels for what feels like days. Bane sleeps as much as he can, wanting to be prepared and well rested for whatever happens. The wagon finally pulls up in front of a small building. He's dragged inside and tossed in a room with another dragonborn, this one a female with bronze scales. She glared at him, saying her name was Pax, and that he'd better stay out of her way.

15 Years Later

Bane charged at the dummy, cleaving it in half with his sword. It turns out that the place Bane had been brought was a gladiator arena. He's now stuck training for life or death flights, where it's kill or be killed.

Pax comes in, rolling her eyes at him. She pulls out her trident, jabbing it through another dummy and throwing it across the room. Despite how she acts Pax has become Bane's bestfriend. They start trading blows, training. Then Teric, the arena master, comes in. He says Bane is up for the next fight. Bane sighs, walking out into the arena.
